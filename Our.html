Our Story

We envision a world where everyone has access to clean drinking water, without single-use plastics.

  • Water-To-Go are experts in water filtration for consumers concerned about getting sick from contaminated water while travelling, hiking, backpacking or just drinking from their home tap.
  • Twelve years ago Water-To-Go developed a water filter bottle that has been used by over half a million people worldwide. By filtering dangerous water contaminants (including viruses, bacteria, parasites, lead, fluoride, chlorine, PFAS, blue-green algae, chemicals, pesticides, and microplastics).
  • One Water-To-Go filter replaces **400 single use plastic bottles** that would otherwise end up in landfill and oceans.
  • We believe in sustainable, eco-friendly travel. We have the only bioplastic water filter bottle for travel, made from sustainably-sourced sugarcane. Our packaging is made from recycled cardboard and is printed with vegetable inks.
  • Everyone deserves safe clean water without single use plastics that pollute our planet. We are helping make that a reality.

Wise adventurers aren't reckless.

We’ve spent hundreds of nights in tents in the backcountry and thousands of days in the developing world. We’ve worked, played and lived in places where having access to safe, clean water is a necessity.

Whether on the trail needing to drink from a dirty stream, filling up at a questionable hotel sink in Peru, or being prepared for an emergency.

Access to Clean Water

Charity: water is an organization that brings clean, safe drinking water to communities in developing countries by building sustainable, community-owned water projects around the world.

This year, Grayl is sponsoring the rehabilitation of a well for a community in Malawi, one of Africa’s most densely populated and least developed countries. The project will provide access to safe drinking water for an entire community in Malawi, drastically reducing the risk of water-related diseases and giving time back to women and children.
